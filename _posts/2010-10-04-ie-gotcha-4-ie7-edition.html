[[posterous-content:GftujlwFpHolACdCAumG]]In an ongoing attempt to alert the general public to all the downfalls you suffer when you use IE I continue the series: IE Gotchas. I haven’t hit any major ones in a while, but this is a weird one.

IE7 (and maybe before?) doesn’t give you the “JSON” object with which to manipulate json objects. Whaaat?

Simple solution is to require a conditional comment to include this file somewhere. Then you’re fine.
