As some may have noticed from the logo in the bottom of this screen, I use Typekit for some font design for my web projects. They are a very cool service adding a lot of “cool-factor” to any web project. Yet another reason to love being a web developer.

And as-per this latest blog post, they just opened up their Portfolio account to use unlimited fonts for unlimited sites. Pretty cool. I had often wondered if I would be able to use them beyond a few sites as I get into more and more freelance work. Very good to know they’ve been concerned with this too. Thanks Typekit! Keep up the amazing work.
