Continuing the rant…

So I already knew IE7 didn’t respect the inline-block property. That sucks. Its really hard to do some types of layout without that property. So what to do?

Believe it or not, this hack works (from flipc.blogspot.com). Just add:

zoom: 1; *display: inline; _height: 30px;

to the end of your styling for the element you need inline-block to work for. It doesn’t screw with Firefox or even IE8 (which handles inline-block just fine…).

So moral of the story is still don’t use IE. Period. Solution one is Mozilla’s Firefox. Solution 2 is at least ie6update.com
